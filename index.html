Personal Meditation Guide

While laying down, closing your eyes. Focus on your breathing and create an awareness within your body of where your awareness is. Bring your awareness to your brain if isn't there already. Become aware of what you are seeing in your 3rd eye. What are you wanting to express? Embracing the feeling that arises within you, begin to feel confident with your desires. Knowing you can pursue them with passion. Now ground this feeling within you as a true foundation of reality.
