Root Chakra (Muladhara)

Creating an Awareness

Focus on the base of your spine. Feel a warm red light glowing at this center. Notice any sensations of heaviness or lightness in your lower body.

Recognizing What You Are Seeing

Visualize a red lotus or spinning wheel of light at the base of your spine. See this energy connecting you to the earth beneath you.

Expressing What You Are Feeling

Acknowledge emotions related to safety, security, or belonging. Notice any feelings of fear, insecurity, or instability, as well as feelings of groundedness, safety, or stability.

Embracing the Feeling as Your Own

Accept your need for security as natural and human. Your desire to feel safe and grounded is valid, even when the world feels chaotic. Embrace both your strength and vulnerability.

Feeling Confident in Your Strength

Affirm: "I am safe and secure in my body. I trust the ground beneath me. I have everything I need to thrive."

Stable Within Your Desires/Passion

Connect with your passion for creating a stable foundation. Feel dedicated to building security that allows spiritual growth to flourish.

Knowing This is Grounded in Reality

Your sense of security creates tangible stability in your life. Your grounded energy manifests as practical support for your spiritual journey.

Root Chakra Meditation

While laying down, closing your eyes. Focus on your breathing and create an awareness within your body of where your awareness is. Bring your awareness to the base of your spine. Become aware of what you are seeing in your mind's eye. What sense of security are you wanting to express? Embracing the feeling that arises within you, begin to feel confident with your stability. Knowing you can build foundations with passion and strength. Now ground this feeling within you as a true foundational reality.
