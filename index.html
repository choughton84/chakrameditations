Chakra Healing Affirmation Journal

Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)

Located at the top of the head, represents spiritual connection and enlightenment.

Affirmation:

I am connected to divine wisdom and universal consciousness.

Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)

Located between the eyebrows, represents intuition and perception.

Affirmation:

I trust my intuition and inner wisdom.

Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

Located at the throat, represents communication and self-expression.

Affirmation:

I express my truth clearly and confidently.

Heart Chakra (Anahata)

Located at the center of the chest, represents love, compassion, and harmony.

Affirmation:

I give and receive love freely and unconditionally.

Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

Located above the navel, represents personal power and confidence.

Affirmation:

I am confident in my personal power and abilities.

Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)

Located below the navel, represents creativity and emotional balance.

Affirmation:

I embrace my creativity and honor my emotions.

Root Chakra (Muladhara)

Located at the base of the spine, represents stability and grounding.

Affirmation:

I am safe, secure, and grounded in the present moment.

Personal Meditation Guide

While laying down, closing your eyes. Focus on your breathing and create an awareness within your body of where your awareness is. Bring your awareness to your brain if isn't there already. Become aware of what you are seeing in your 3rd eye. What are you wanting to express? Embracing the feeling that arises within you, begin to feel confident with your desires. Knowing you can pursue them with passion. Now ground this feeling within you as a true foundation al reality.

Chakra Meditation Steps:

  1. CREATING AN AWARENESS
  2. RECOGNIZING WHAT YOU ARE SEEING
  3. EXPRESSING WHAT YOU ARE FEELING
  4. EMBRACING THE FEELING AS YOUR OWN
  5. FEELING CONFIDANT IN YOUR STRENGTH
  6. STABILE WITHIN YOUR DESIRES/PASSION
  7. KNOWING THIS IS GROUNDED IN REALITY
