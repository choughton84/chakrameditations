Chakra Meditations

Root Chakra Meditation

The Root Chakra is located at the base of the spine and is associated with feelings of safety, security, and groundedness.

Meditation: Focus on your connection to the earth. Visualize roots extending from the base of your spine deep into the ground, anchoring you and providing stability.

While laying down, closing your eyes. Focus on your breathing and create an awareness within your body of where your awareness is. Bring your awareness to your base if it isn't there already. Become aware of what you are seeing in your root chakra. What are you wanting to express? Embracing the feeling that arises within you, begin to feel confident with your desires. Knowing you can pursue them with passion. Now ground this feeling within you as a true foundational reality.
